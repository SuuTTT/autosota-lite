## Metadata fields

- Title: required; inline and block TeX formulas supported.
- Authors: required; all authors need OpenReview profiles before submission.
- TL;DR: short sentence; no explicit maximum was visible in the captured text.

- Secondary topics: at least one and at most five.

## Upload channels

| Channel | Required | Captured constraint |
|---|---|---|
| Main PDF | Yes | Paper's main content in PDF format |
| Reproducibility Checklist | Yes | Answered standalone checklist from the AAAI-27 Author Kit |
| Technical Supplement | No | Main paper must stand alone; reviewers need not consider it; maximum 10 MB |
| Media Supplement | No | Images, audio, video, animations, or other multimedia; maximum 50 MB |
| Code and Data Supplement | No | Archive of source, scripts, data, and instructions; maximum 50 MB |

**Hard rule:** linking the paper's sources or data in an external code/data
repository is forbidden, including anonymized repositories such as
AnonymousGitHub. Put reviewer-facing artifacts in the uploaded archive.

## Reciprocal reviewer

The nominee must not be an SPC, AC, or organizer and must have either at least two
first-author publications or at least five co-authored publications in qualifying
peer-reviewed archival venues related to AAAI. Workshop papers do not count. Use
the official AAAI Reviewer Qualification Checker and DBLP.

The nominee agrees to PC service and a standard load of up to six papers if
assigned. The form warns that the submission may be desk rejected when a qualified
author is available but not nominated, or when the nominee does not complete the
assigned reviews.

A separate DBLP URL field is available when the nominee's OpenReview profile cannot
store the correct DBLP page because it is a disambiguation page. The required
confirmation is either:

- a qualified author has been nominated; or
- no author qualifies, with acknowledgement of desk-rejection risk if false.

## Policy acknowledgements

Authors attest that:

- every author profile has the full publication name, current position,
  institution-affiliated email, and DBLP URL if available;
- the manuscript or a substantially similar version is not under review at another
  archival venue;
- relevant simultaneous submissions by any author are anonymously cited as
  `under review` work.
