# Portfolio consistency without uniform prose

When polishing several papers, share the quality system but not a phrasebank.

Share claim-ledger format, evidence and uncertainty rules, related-paper palette/notation, citation/anonymity/submission checks, and overlap/source-data audits.

Keep contribution-family narrative, technical nouns and mechanism verbs, author-written cadence, limitations, interpretation, and visual grammar paper-specific.

A portfolio where every abstract uses the same transitions, three-part list, sentence lengths, and closing formula is less credible and less readable. Use aggregate models to detect outliers, not to stamp ten papers with one voice.
